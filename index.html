About Rotaract

Rooted in the guiding principle of Service Above Self, Rotaract is a global movement that unites young people in the pursuit of sustainable development and meaningful change. With a diverse network of students and professionals, it fosters leadership, collaboration, and a passion for community service.

Members grow as leaders by planning impactful events, leading service projects, and driving initiatives in areas such as environmental sustainability, education, and healthcare. At its heart, Rotaract is about purposeful action—empowering individuals to give back, create real impact, and make a lasting difference in their communities and beyond.

About District 3206

Rotaract District 3206, headquartered in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant network of young leaders committed to the motto “See Beyond, Serve Ahead”. Operating under Rotary International, the district unites students and professionals to drive impactful initiatives in healthcare, education, environmental sustainability, and community development. Guided by a dedicated leadership team-including District Governor Rtn. MD. Chella K. Raghavendran, District Rotaract Chair Rtn. MD. Fredrick John, District Rotaract Representative Rtr. Gogul, and DRR-Elect Rtr. MPHF. Vijay Vignesh VK–District 3206 fosters leadership, collaboration, and service among its members.

With a strong presence in Coimbatore and surrounding regions, it connects to the global Rotaract family of over 1.2 million members in more than 200 countries, offering opportunities for cultural exchange, professional growth, and international fellowship. Every project undertaken reflects the district's dedication to Service Above Self, empowering young people to create meaningful change in their communities and beyond.

Our Club

Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Changemakers is a newly chartered (2025) community-based Rotaract Club operating under Rotary International District 3206. Based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, the club is parented by the Rotary E-Club of Coimbatore Changemakers.

The club was chartered in 2025 with Rtr. Vishnushankar serving as its Charter President for the year 2025–26. As a community-based club, it welcomes members from diverse professional, academic, and entrepreneurial backgrounds, offering opportunities for service, leadership development, and fellowship.
